## Overview

The **Cells tiling** is the dual of the [Shield tiling](/ifs/shield-tiling), obtained by
projecting the same 4D rational space onto the **second eigenplane** (`$subspace=[s,1]`)
instead of the first. In the cut-and-project scheme, this is equivalent to swapping the
roles of direct space and internal space.

The tiling shares the **12-fold** rotational symmetry and the same inflation factor
$|g|^2 = 2+\sqrt{3}$ as the Shield tiling. The algebraic description uses inverse maps:
$h_k^{-1}g$ instead of $g^{-1}h_k$.

## Dual GIFS Structure

The four attractor types $A_0, A_1, A_2, A_3$ satisfy inverse-map GIFS equations:

$$
A_0 = h_9^{-1}g(A_3) \cup \cdots \cup h_{15}^{-1}g(A_3) \cup h_4^{-1}g(A_2) \cup h_5^{-1}g(A_2) \cup h_6^{-1}g(A_2) \cup h_7^{-1}g(A_2)
$$

$$
A_3 = h_0^{-1}g(A_0)
$$

The dependency structure is: $A_3 \to A_0 \to A_3$ (cyclic), plus links to $A_1$ and $A_2$.
